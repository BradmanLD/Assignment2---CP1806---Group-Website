Goals

The goal of the website is to increase the number of attendees at Barrier Reef Orchestra concerts & events.

Success Evaluation

The site's success will be determined by the number of new members that sign up through the website, and an increase in the number of attendees and/or ticket sales for orchestra concerts & events.

Target Audience

The target auidence of the website is young adults aged 15 - 30 interested in orchestral music. The website will be designed to appeal to this auidence by having facebook integration, using lighter colors (Blues, Whites, etc). The current comps were influenced by the designs of the State Youth Orchestra websites (i.e. QSO, ASO).
