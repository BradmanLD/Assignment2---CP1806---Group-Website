Notice Of AGM (Annual General Meeting)

  • Post By: nqEnsemblesAdmin
  • AUGUST, 05, 2014

Overview

All members are advised that the Annual General Meeting, (AGM), of North Queensland will take place at Pimlico State High School Performing Arts Building, Fulham Road, Pimlico on Sunday 17th of May 2015, commencing at 2pm.

The following points should be noted:

  • Membership renewals are due by 31st of January 2015. Renewal Notices will be sent to members with the January 2015 issue of BRO News.
  • Nominations are called for the positions of: President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer, and for seven Committee Members. Nomination forms can be found in the PDF. Nominations close May 8th.
  • Members are urged to consider making themselves available for Committee membership.

Agenda - AGM 2015

  • OPENING: 2PM
  • OPENING REMARKS
  • ATTENDANCE & APOLOGIES
  • LODGEMENT OF PROXY VOTES
  • MINUTES OF PREVIOUS AGM
  • BUSINESS REGARDING MINUTES
  • PRESIDENT'S 2014 REPORT
  • TREASURER'S REPORT
  • GOVERNOR'S ADDRESS
  • ELECTION OF OFFICE BEARERS
  • APPOINTMENT OF 2014 AUDITOR
  • CLOSING REMARKS
