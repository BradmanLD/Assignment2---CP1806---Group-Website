Life Members

Alan Nixon

Life Member Since May 2015

Alan has been Secretary of North Queensland Ensembles Inc. / Barrier Reef Orchestra for eight years. His expertise as a former Commonwealth Government officer served the Barrier Reef Orchestra well.

Jacinta Payne

Life Member Since May 2015

Jacinta played Clarinet at the very first Orchestra event on Sunday 29th October 2000. Over the years, through her teaching she has been able to encourage many new players to join the Orchestra and has mentored many woodwind players.

Stephen Frewen-Lord

Life Member Since May 2015

Stephen was the concert master at the Orchestra's inaugural concert in 2000. Since then, he has had many discussions with guest conductors and artists concering the artistic direction of the orchestra.

Jenny Carr

Life Member Since July 2010

Jenny Carr has been an integral part of the Townsville music scene as a music teach since the 70s. As the orchestra was being created Jenny worked with others to arrange a series of concerts for the emerging orchestra.

Carol Dall'Osto

Life Member Since July 2010

Carol has been a leading figure in music performances in Townsville for many years. She was a contributor to the formation of the Barrier Reef Orchestra, and was chairperson of the North Queensland Ensembles Committee.

Donna McMahon

Life Member Since July 2010

Donna has been a leader in the Townsville music community since 1975. She was a key contributor to the formation of the Barrier Reef Orchestra. She has also been a performer, rehearsal conductor, musical advisor, & visting conductor liason.

David Pearse

Life Member Since June 2006

David was heavily involved with the Townsville music scene from the 1970s, including the formation of the Barrier Reef Orchestra in 1999.
