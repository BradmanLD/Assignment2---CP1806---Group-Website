Adam Lopez

Musician - Vocals

About Adam Lopez

Adam Lopez's has performed backing vocals for famous artists such as Mariah Carey, Keith Urban, and The Coors. His most recent noted collaboration was headlining with the Australian Jazz legend James Morrison in a string of Symphonic Concerts in Perth, Hobart, and Brisbane.

His discography includes an eclectic collection of genres spanning Pop-Opera, Pop, Rhythm & Blues, Jazz, and Power Ballards.

Adam Lopez's vocal range allow him to achieve two Guinness World Records (one not above the range of a piano). This achievement has led him to performing all over the world including countries such as Japan, Spain, Italy, Korea, and the USA
