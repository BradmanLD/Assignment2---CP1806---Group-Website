Bronwyn Douglas

Vocalist

About Bronwyn Douglas

Bronwyn is a Mezzo-soprano from Milton, NSW. Bronwyn graduated with a Masters in Operatice Performance from the Queensland Conservatorium in 2014, also receiving an award for Academic Excellence and the Wagner Society Encouragement Award. She attended the Lisa Gasteen National Opera School from 2013 - 2015 as well as undertaking the Mediterranean Opera Studio Programme held in Caltigirone Sicily

Bronwyn has received many awards, grants and scholarships. In 2016 alone she was awarded the 3rd place DECCA award in the Joan Sutherland and Richard Bonynge Bel Canto Award, 2nd place in the Opera and Arts Support Group Vocal Scholarship and was a finalist in the Sydney Eisteddfod Opera Scholarship. In 2015 she won the Australian Concerto and Vocal Competetion getting her invited to perform as a soloist with the Barrier Reef Orchestra.
