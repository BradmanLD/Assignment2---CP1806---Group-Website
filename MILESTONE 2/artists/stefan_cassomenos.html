Stefen Cassomenos

Musician - Piano

About Stefen Cassomenos

Melbourne pianist, conductor and composer Stefan Cassomenos gives regular solo recitals throughout Australia, and has appeared as a soloist in Europe and Asia. He performed the premiere of his own first piano concerto with the Adelaide Symphony Orchestra at the age of 16, and directed the 2010 Melbourne Recictal Centre premiere of his third piano concerto. He studied under with Margarita Krupina and subsequently with Stephen McIntyre on scholarship at the University of Melbourne.

In 2012 he was grand finalist in the Rhodes International Piano Competition, and his recording of English and Australian art song was named the ABC Calssic FM CD of the week. Additionally, he was the grand finalist for and prize-winner at both the Trieste International Chamber Music Competition and the Melbourne Asia-Pacific Chamber Music Competition in 2009.

He has worked as an artistic director of many festivals, projects and collaborations including the Melba Festival and Melbourne's Piano Landmakrs festival. The compositions of Stefan Cassomenos have been played by the Melbourne and Adelaide Symphony Orchestras.
