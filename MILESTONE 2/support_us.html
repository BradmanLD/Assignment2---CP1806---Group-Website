Methods To Support Us

Become a Member

Support the Orchestra by donating a set amount of money to become a member.

Membership Costs

  • Adult: $30
  • Family: $60
  • Full time Student: $15
  • Under 18: $15
  • Concession: $15

Become a Benefactor

A donation of $200 or more will allow anyone to become a benefactor of the Orchestra.

Benefactor Perks:

  • Name on the benefactors roll for twelve months
  • Two tickets to our next event
  • Updates via our newsletter

Join the Orchestra

As a community based Orchestra we are always looking for new members of the community to join us. We take community members on as musicians, fundraisers, and many other positions. If you feel that a position you could fill is not on the form please contact us via brotownsville@gmail.com and let us know how you would like to help.
