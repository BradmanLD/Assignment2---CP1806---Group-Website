Methods To Support Us

Become A Member

Support the Orchestra by donating a set amount of money to become a member.

Membership Costs

  • Adult: $30
  • Family: $60
  • Full time Student: $15
  • Under 18: $15
  • Concession: $15

Become a Benefactor

A donation of $200 or more will allow anyone to become a benefactor of the Orchestra.

The benefits of being a benefactor:

  • Have your name put into the Benefactors roll for twelve months
  • Receive two complimentary tickets to our next concert
  • Receive updates on the Orchestra via our newsletter
  • Be a guest at our annual Season's Launch function.

If submitting using the PDF form, mail it to the PO Box 576 Townsville 4810, and make cheques payable to North Queensland Ensembles Inc. or Email it to brotownsville@gmail.com and pay direct debit to BSB 034 22 Account no. 173922.

Join the Orchestra

As a community based Orchestra we are always looking for new members of the community to join us. Whether it be as musicians or fundraisers, etc.
